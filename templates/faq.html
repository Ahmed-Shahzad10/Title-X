FAQ

Questions sellers usually ask

What does Title-X do?

It generates 10 Daraz-ready product title ideas from your product details, supplier copy, or rough notes.

Does it write full descriptions?

This version focuses on product titles only, so sellers can quickly improve listing discoverability.

What should I paste into the tool?

Include the supplier title, product type, features, color, size, material, quantity, and any buyer keywords you want to target.

Are the results final?

Use them as strong starting points. Pick the clearest option and adjust for exact product facts before publishing.
